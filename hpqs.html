Continuous Diligence Process

Proprietary Risk Management Platform.

A system for streamlining the path from the initial feasibility studies to an offtake agreement.

Together with our technical partners, we develop region-specific risk-management algorithms and models to monitor and evaluate every module in a green hydrogen value chain.

This system measures the risk, competitiveness, and reliability of a green hydrogen production project based on the information produced and collected during, but not limited to, the feasibility and engineering design phases, business plans, consortia formation, infrastructure plans, and technological decisions.

Through this platform, we have a multi-stage evaluation process that drives our partnerships with production projects transparently and predictably, from feasibility studies to first-molecule.

Our mission is to support the best projects by providing future commercial certainties early in their lifecycle. Our technology is our way of defining that future.
