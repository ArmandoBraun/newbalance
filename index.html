Green Hydrogen Commerce

From 2025, a full global roll out of CO2-free hydrogen will be required to transition today’s hydrogen global demand to one free from CO2.

NewBalance was designed to identify, build, scale-up, and repurpose existing developments across the renewable energy and industrial sectors to enable a reliable production and distribution of green hydrogen at an unmatched cost.
